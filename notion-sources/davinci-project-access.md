Step 2
Download the latest version of DaVinci Resolve or DaVinci Resolve Studio if you have the license. (For Color Graders you are going to need Resolve Studio)
Support Center | Blackmagic Design
Blackmagic Design customer support provides the latest software updates, support notes, instruction manuals and contacts for any product related questions.
https://www.blackmagicdesign.com/support/family/davinci-resolve-and-fusion
For most folk, Resolve will get you 95% the way there

Step 3
Resolve Studio is the paid version of the app, it looks exactly the same but with more powerful features
Join the organization
Contact an admin (LK, Zayy, Sayo, or JulieChi) to add you to invite you to the Public Worship Organization, and be added to the "Editors" Group. Make sure to send them the same email as you Blackmagic cloud account

💡 Template
Hey _, do you mind adding me to the PW Organization and the Editors Group? Here is the link where you can get it done, Here is my email me@gmail.com
https://apps.cloud.blackmagicdesign.com/organizations/org_SxUWvRCz5BbfGzxT6oRBxp/members
You should get an email invite, once you accept the invite, notify the admin so they can add you to the editors group

Step 4
Open up DaVinci on your computer
Click on cloud and log in to your Blackmagic cloud account from step 1, and choose the public worship profile
Now you should be able to see all of our projects and create new projects 🙂
