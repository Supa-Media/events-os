🔪 Editing Prep

Author(s): @Seyi Olujide @Layomi Kupoluyi
Last Updated: @July 29, 2025

Thank you for taking the time to prep an edit, it truly is super helpful in streamlining the editing process. Before following this doc, make sure that you have 🔓 DaVinci Project Access

For Editing prep the goals are to
Uploading and Arranging Files: Have all the project clips and files uploaded to the cloud, and arranged into proper bins
Master Timeline Creation: Syncing all the clips together in the master timeline, arranging all the clips in the appropriate tracks

Uploading and Arranging Files

Step 1: Copy the template
If you have properly gotten 🔓 DaVinci Project Access, you can see all our current projects, Create a copy of the template, and rename it in the regular convention (Event - Lead Person)
Make a copy of template (5.23 MB)
📖 Make sure you sync proxies and originals

Step 2: Upload Clips
Upload both the Video and Audio to the Drop box under Areas > Media
Duplicate the project template folder and re-name the duplicated folder to the relevant project name. See the video below:
Uploading video to dropbox.mp4 — Shared with Dropbox
https://www.dropbox.com/scl/fi/t4gjm48qadpidlmemugny/Uploading-video-to-dropbox.mp4?rlkey=yj50ex8wrs258ay0ktzpvprgw&st=gbu9g27m&dl=0

For audio Usually we record audio on a Zoom audio recorder. Find the one used to record the session (Usually in the production bag, or rack), and get out the micro SD card. Plug the SD card into its adapter (Usually in the production bag, or rack)

❗ If we recorded audio directly on the camera, no need to use the audio recorder

plug in the SD card directly into the SD card slot on a macbook pro, or by using the SD card dongle on an air. Feel free to buy a dongle if you need one 💳 Need a Company Card

For video you can ask the person on camera which SD card the footage was recorded to, and use that

Now upload the clips in the respective places, be sure to upload everything captured from that day. All audio clips can be put in the same bin, but lets try our best to separate the Camera clips by which camera they are from.

Uploading Files (13.18 MB)

Master Timeline Creation

Now we want to create a master arrangement inside the project. There are many ways to go about this, but the end result should be the same. A very long 1-3 hour timeline with all the clips synced. This is how I recommend going about it

Open up the Master Timeline
Put the audio files down first. Usually Audio is recording the whole day, the tricky part about audio is that it is usually separated out into individual mics and channels. So make sure you listen to it and put in in the proper track
Now do the same for all the video clips, feel free to unmute the tracks in order to align by listening to the audio
